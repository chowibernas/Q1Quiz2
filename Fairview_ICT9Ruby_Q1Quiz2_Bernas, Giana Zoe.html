Princess and the Frog



The young and ambitious Tiana dreams of opening a restaurant until she met a frog who claims to be Prince Naveen under a spell. To break the spell, a princess must plant a kiss on the frog for him to return back to his original form. It backfired, turning Tiana into one of his own species. The frog duo continues to venture into the forest in order to seek the assistance of a voodoo priestess while avoiding Dr. Facilier, the man responsible for the evil amphibian deeds.


Characters:


Tiana (Anika Noni Rose)
    Prince Naveen (Bruno Campos)
    Dr. Facilier (Keitch David)
